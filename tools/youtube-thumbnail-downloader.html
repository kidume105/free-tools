YouTube Thumbnail Downloader – Download YouTube Thumbnails

Get thumbnail images from public YouTube video URLs.

This gets the thumbnail image only, not the video file.

How do I get a YouTube thumbnail?

Paste a valid YouTube video URL and click get thumbnail.

Does this download the video?

No. It only extracts the thumbnail image from a public video URL.

Can I download high-quality thumbnails?

Yes. The tool tries the highest available image and falls back if needed.
