YouTube Thumbnail Downloader – Download YouTube Thumbnails

Extract and download YouTube thumbnail images in high quality from public video URLs.

This gets the thumbnail image only, not the video file.

How do I get a YouTube thumbnail?

Paste a valid YouTube video URL, then click the get thumbnail button.

Does this download the video?

No. This tool only extracts the thumbnail image from a public YouTube URL.

Can I download high-quality thumbnails?

Yes, the tool tries to use the highest available thumbnail and falls back when needed.
